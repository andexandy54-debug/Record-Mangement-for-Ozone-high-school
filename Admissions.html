Admissions

Ozone High School welcomes students who are committed to academic excellence, discipline, and personal growth.
admissions process is designed to ensure that every student is prepared to succeed in a supportive and structured learning environment.

Applicants are required to submit previous academic records, complete the official admission form, and meet the school's academic requirements. Admission decisions are based on academic performance, conduct, and available space.

Once admitted, student information is securely registered in our Record Management System to ensure accurate tracking of academic progress, attendance, and performance reports.

For more information about enrollment dates, requirements, and procedures, please contact the school administration office.
